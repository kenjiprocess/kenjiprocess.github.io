About the Distributor

Supplier-first logistics with engineer-level context

PROCESS AUTOMATION SERVICES LLC is a wholesale distributor of durable industrial automation products — PLCs, VFDs, HMI panels, safety devices, pneumatics, sensors, cabling, and control enclosures — serving manufacturers, utilities, and infrastructure contractors. From our operations hub at 1600 Ponce De Leon Blvd, Ste 1000, Coral Gables, FL 33134, we ship nationwide with time-definite options.

We are vendor-neutral and inventory both tier-one and niche brands, allowing us to recommend parts that fit your installed base, not just a single line card. Our team includes former controls engineers who translate specs into stocked SKUs and propose drop-in replacements when supply is tight. We also build custom UL-listed control panels in small series and offer kitted material for integrators.

Compliance and safety are non-negotiable. We maintain RoHS/REACH documentation, provide certificates of conformance on request, and track lot numbers for recalls. Every product ships in protective, moisture-barrier packaging with anti-static handling where applicable.

Above all, we design our wholesale workflows around uptime: fast quoting, live availability, and proactive alternates when a line is constrained. If you need it on the shelf tomorrow, we plan it today.

Our Solutions

Our Playbook

Simple three-step path from spec to shipment

1) Scope & Match

Send the part list or a napkin sketch. We match to stocked SKUs, verify compatibility, and propose alternates where lead times are risky.

2) Confirm & Kit

We lock specs, pre-configure firmware and parameters when requested, and kit by installation area to reduce site handling.

3) Ship & Support

Same-day pick for in-stock lines, milestone notifications, and an engineer on call for first-power assistance.

Service Levels

Standard (1–3 days), Expedited (next-day), and Critical (same-day/hot-shot in South FL). International export available with ITAR screening.

All shipments include ESD-safe handling where applicable and shock/tilt indicators for sensitive drives and optics.

Quality & Compliance

UL 508A for panel shops, CE guidance, RoHS/REACH tracking, CoC on request, and careful vendor vetting. We prioritize authenticated supply chains.

For safety gear, we track batch data and expiration where relevant (e.g., gas sensors) and maintain recall readiness.
